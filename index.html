About Us

Surat's Trusted
Manufacturer

Shree Hari Engineers is a Surat-based manufacturer specializing in high carbon washers and fastening components. With years of expertise in precision manufacturing, we deliver products that meet demanding industrial standards.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Dwarkesh Industrial Estate enables us to handle bulk orders while maintaining consistent quality. We serve clients across construction, automotive, and heavy machinery sectors — domestically and internationally.

Every washer that leaves our facility undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure dimensional accuracy, material integrity, and surface finish.
